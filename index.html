About Us

Green-tech Visionary Enterprise Limited is a Nigerian innovation company creating AI-powered and IoT-integrated solutions across agriculture, health, education, commerce, finance, energy, and oil & gas. Our mission is to solve Africa's pressing challenges through scalable, sustainable, and impact-driven platforms.

We operate on core values of innovation, impact, sustainability, and collaboration.

  • Innovation: Pioneering cutting-edge solutions for Africa's challenges
  • Impact: Creating measurable positive change in communities
  • Sustainability: Developing solutions that are environmentally and economically sustainable
  • Collaboration: Partnering with stakeholders across sectors for maximum impact
